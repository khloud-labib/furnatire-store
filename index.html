What is the difference between us and others

Design quality, quality of materials, and punctuality of delivery

Design quality

is the quality which the producer or supplier is intending to offer to the customer. When the producer is making the quality of design of the product, he should take into consideration the customer's requirements in order to satisfy them with fitness for use of the product

quality of materials

means, with respect to any product, package, label, specification or commercial material containing any product claim, any and all information and materials regarding the evaluation of such product, package, label, specification or material

punctuality of delivery

measuring the rate of finished product and deliveries made in time. This rate is expressed in a total number of units delivered within a set period defined by the customer and the supplier.
